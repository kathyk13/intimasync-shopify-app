## Description (for Shopify App Store listing page)

IntimaSync connects your Shopify store to leading intimacy product distributors, automating catalog sync, inventory management, and order fulfillment. Stop manually placing orders with suppliers. IntimaSync routes every order to the supplier with the best price and available stock, then submits it for dropship fulfillment automatically.

**Supported Suppliers:**
- Honey's Place
- Eldorado Trading Company
- Nalpac (including Entrenue catalog)

**What IntimaSync Does:**
- Syncs full product catalogs from connected suppliers into a searchable browser
- Imports products to your Shopify store as drafts with images, descriptions, and UPCs
- Matches existing Shopify products to supplier records by UPC barcode
- Keeps inventory levels in sync with real-time supplier stock
- Routes incoming orders to the cheapest supplier with available stock
- Consolidates multi-item orders to reduce split shipments
- Submits orders to suppliers via their APIs for hands-free fulfillment
- Tracks order status and shipping from a single dashboard

**Plans:**
- Starter ($29/mo): 1 supplier, daily inventory sync, email support
- Growth ($59/mo): 3 suppliers, custom sync schedule, priority support
- Pro ($99/mo): Unlimited suppliers, real-time inventory, dedicated account manager

All plans include a 14-day free trial.
